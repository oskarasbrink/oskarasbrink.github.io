Projects

GenJSONnotebookGrader

An AutoGrader for notebooks in JSON-format and Currently supports notebooks in Jupyter and Databricks formats. It fetches assignment from Canvas, converts them to JSON-format, grades them in a Docker envirnonment for Jupyter and on Databricks for dbc notebooks. After sucessful grading the assignments are automatically uploaded to Canvas with its respective grade. My main part in this was combining projects for the handling of the notebooks, autograder and Canvas communication into one package, and refactoring and cleaning/OOP-ification of the whole project. I also continued the development of the system for Databricks notebooks and added some fetures related to them. Support for Zeppelin notebooks is also under development.
