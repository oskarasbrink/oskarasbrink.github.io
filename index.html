General JSON notebook AutoGrader

This GenJSONNotebookGrader is a general AutoGrader for notebooks in Ipynb and Databricks format. My main tasks are bugfixes, adding functionalities, rewriting code and refactoring and restructuring the whole project. This is still in early development, and is intended to be easily implemented and scaleable to other courses. This AutoGrader is currently used in two courses at Uppsala University. It is able to fetch student submissions from the learning management system Canvas , add extra cells to the notbook to check student solutions when run, and then grade it locally or on a Databricks cluster depending on the format of the notebook. Then remove the traces of the Testing, convert it back to its original format submit to Canvas with a grade and feedback. This was presented and demonstated to Databricks University Alliance in late september 2022.

Full time Data Science project at Trase.earth

The project also includes analysis and predictions related with the commodities transfers to temporary supply sheds, and tracking movement of cattle in the process from birth to slaughter to help meat processing plants see which cattle are tied to recently deforested areas.
